Providing Exceptional Healthcare Services Since 1986

Established in 1986, our hospital is committed to providing exceptional healthcare services at an affordable cost. Built on a foundation of ethical practices, we prioritize the wellbeing of our patients and their families.

Discover Our Specialties

Our specialties include General Medicine, Orthopedics, Obstetrics and Gynecology, along with General and Laparoscopic Surgeries. Additionally, we offer comprehensive care in the field of ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat).

Discover Our Commitment to Quality Care

Over the decades, we have continuously evolved to meet the changing health needs of our community while maintaining our commitment to cost-effective, ethical care. Trust us to provide you with the care you deserve.

Annual Health Camps: Serving the Community

In addition to our regular services, we are proud to host annual health camps. These events embody our commitment to serving the community, particularly those most in need. During these camps, we offer free medications and health tests, ensuring that everyone has access to essential healthcare services, regardless of their financial circumstances. Our mission to care for our community extends beyond our hospital walls, and we are dedicated to making a positive impact wherever we can.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • C-sections or Normal delivery ?

    Choosing between a C-section and a normal delivery is a personal decision best made in consultation with your doctor. Here's some information to get you started:

    Delivery: Preferred for low-risk pregnancies, offering faster recovery times and potential health benefits for both mother and baby.

    C-section: A surgical procedure used when a normal delivery is not safe for mother or baby. Reasons for C-sections can include breech presentation, multiples, complications during labor, or health concerns for the mother.

    We recommend discussing your birthing plan and any concerns with your doctor to determine the safest and most suitable delivery method for you. The decision should be made in consultation with your doctor.

  • Laparoscopic Surgeries?

    Laparoscopic surgeries are minimally invasive procedures performed using small incisions and a laparoscope (a thin, lighted instrument with a camera). Laparoscopy is a common surgical technique used for various procedures, including gallbladder removal, appendectomies, hernia repairs, and gynecological surgeries.

    Benefits of laparoscopy include:

    Reduced scarring
    Shorter hospital stays
    Faster recovery times
    Less pain

    Laparoscopy can be used for various procedures, including:

    Gallbladder removal
    Appendectomy
    Hernia repair
    Gynecological surgeries

    Consult your doctor to see if laparoscopy is suitable for your specific surgery.
  • Orthopedics Surgeries?

    Orthopedic surgery focuses on the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons. Our hospital offers a wide range of orthopedic surgeries, including:

    Joint replacement (knee, hip, shoulder)
    Arthroscopy (minimally invasive joint surgery)
    Fracture repair
    Ligament reconstruction
    Spine surgery

    information on specific procedures, please visit our "Orthopedic Services" section (link on website) or consult your doctor.
  • Knee/Hip Replacement?

    Knee and hip replacement surgeries are performed to replace damaged joints with prosthetic implants. This can significantly improve mobility and reduce pain.

    Our hospital offers advanced techniques and implants for successful knee and hip replacements. Consult your doctor to determine if joint replacement is right for you.
  • Rooms, IPD Facility for Fevers?

    We offer comfortable and well-equipped rooms for In-Patient Department (IPD) stays. While we specialize in a variety of procedures, it's important to note that fevers are often managed on an outpatient basis.

    Please contact our emergency department or your doctor to determine the best course of action for a fever.
